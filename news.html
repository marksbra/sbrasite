Pub Quiz Night (Irish-Themed Fun Quiz) Wednesday March 4th 2026

When: Wednesday 4th March, 8:30pm

Where: The Wren’s Nest, Strawberry Beds

Tables of 4. Do come along and we can find a team for you.

Cost: €10 per person for quiz entry, plus a raffle on the night. We’ll be taking cash and cards, so don’t forget your wallets.

It would be great to see as many of you as possible as we raise funds for various initiatives within the Strawberry Beds, and it’s a chance to get together.

If any resident or local business would like to donate a spot prize, please reach out to a member of the committee.
